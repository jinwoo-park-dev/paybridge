Recording sequence

  1. Backend creates the PaymentIntent and returns a client secret.
  2. Stripe-hosted fields collect card details and confirm the payment in-browser.
  3. Return-page verification records the payment; webhook processing adds asynchronous confirmation and duplicate protection.
